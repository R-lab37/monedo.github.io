About Us

Monido is a non-governmental organisation that works tirelessly in the areas of education, health, and community development, which we think are the most in need and ought to be the linchpins of our attention. Education, health, child development, water and sanitation, women's empowerment, and gender equality are all important in the community, and providing "free of charge" humanitarian services to society's differently abled and needy members, is the basic building block in an effort to achieve these goals. We think that the trinity of education, health, and community development are closely intertwined and that working together is the only way to create a long-term and significant change in the beneficiary population, especially for underprivileged children and women. Our work as a holistic grassroots organisation working with marginalised communities has earned us peer acclaim and recognition as one of the top 10 NGOs in India from a wide range of sources.

Our Journey

As a non profit organisation we believe that any program or initiative should be built on a productive relationship that leads to trust the basis of transformation. As a result we not only focus on change in and of itself but we also believe that how change is achieved is just as essential as the change itself. Local solutions built in close cooperation with the people themselves, which enhance the impact and encourage empowerment, are one of the most successful strategies to accomplish long-term, transformative change. As a not-for-profit organisation, we believe in an integrated strategy that addresses population-suffering challenges from all perspectives in measurable and practical ways. This should be accomplished by empowering people by increasing their capacity to live a good life, earn a decent income, and profit from the sustainable use and management of the resources available to them.

Community-Based Organisation

In an era where "inclusive development" is being emphasised as the way to long term growth, targeted actions for the welfare of the handicapped are critical. From its beginnings the foundation's primary area of focus was on people with poor child education because education make people self reliant.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build an inclusive society in which people with disabilities are fully integrated into all aspects of economic and social life. We think that everyone is unique and brilliant and that all they need is a chance to shine. We work on altering behaviours and practices at the grassroots level and influencing public policy at the systemic level with the support of fundraising for non-profits like us, so we can establish an environment in which the impoverished are considered the nation's priority.

Our Values

We work for people who is poor to promote equal rights and opportunities.we empower organisations and communities to create realistic and long term solutions.
